About Me

Hi, I’m Jen, an embroidery artist and pattern designer living in Bristol.

After graduating from the Arts University Bournemouth with a BA Hons in Textile Design I established myself as a pattern designer, screen printer and embroidery artist.
I have created engaging design solutions for both client briefs and personal projects, including a collection of handmade jewellery and accessories in addition to a catalogue of ready made patterns.
Having recently moved back to the UK after living in Berlin, Germany for many years I now focus on embroidery art and create hand embroidered pet portraits on a commission basis.
